About God's Will Charity Centre

Our Mission and Vision

Founded in 2020, God's Will Charity Centre is a faith-based orphanage dedicated to providing a loving home, quality education, and holistic care for orphaned and vulnerable children. Our mission is to nurture each child in our care with love, compassion, and Christian values, preparing them for a brighter future.

We currently provide a safe and nurturing environment for over 35 children, ranging from infants to teenagers. Our dedicated staff and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every child receives proper nutrition, medical care, education, and emotional support.

At God's Will Charity Centre, we believe every child is a precious gift from God, deserving of love, dignity, and the opportunity to reach their full potential. We partner with communities, churches, and compassionate individuals to transform lives and break the cycle of poverty.

Our Programs

Education Support

We ensure all children in our care attend school and receive the academic support they need. This includes tuition fees, school supplies, uniforms, and after-school tutoring programs.

Healthcare Services

Our children receive regular medical check-ups, vaccinations, dental care, and immediate treatment when ill. We also provide nutritional meals and mental health support.

Family Living Environment

Instead of dormitory-style living, we maintain a family model with house parents who provide personalized care, creating a more nurturing home environment.

Community Outreach

We believe that every individual deserves to live with dignity and hope, regardless of their circumstances. Our Community Outreach program is dedicated to serving the most vulnerable members of our society - the widows, the disabled, children from underprivileged families, and those struggling to make ends meet.

We strive to provide emotional support, practical assistance, and a listening ear to those who need it most. Our goal is to empower them to overcome their challenges, rebuild their lives, and regain their confidence.
